Artist Statement

As a creator, I am driven by a longing need to disintegrate my interals to the world outside of me. My work is greatly influenced by creation, being, and destruction. I started years ago in the realm of sonics. Molding worlds where ears can live for split seconds and heartbeats can race. Now from various pivots I reside in poetry..
